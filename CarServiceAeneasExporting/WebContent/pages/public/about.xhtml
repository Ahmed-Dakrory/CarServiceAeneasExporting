Aeneas Exporting is a professional, reliable and reputable LLC specializing in exporting vehicle and consumable food products to Africa.

We pride ourselves in using some of the best and most reliable ports in the United States: Newark, NJ, Savannah, GA., Houston, TX and Compton, CA.

Our dedicated team of specialists have mastered the art of exporting in a timely manner to ensure the utmost quality of professionalism that is expected from our customers. Aeneas team members are often addressing, and conquering, unique situations behind the scenes to ensure a smooth and enjoyable exchange for customers. We take pride in our work, customer service skills and attention to detail. That is what makes Aeneas Exporting a company that works for you, our customer.
