OUR SERVICES

Container

Convenient and Economical

Containerized shipments are the most convenient and economical way of moving your goods across the world. once a booking is confirmed shipping lines will provide a container where your good will be loaded on the vessel. Once the load is completed the container will be returned to port to clear US Customs and be loaded on the vessel. Containers most commonly used are available in the following sizes but not all size are available on all services and all shipping lines (20 ft standard, 40 ft standard, 40 ft High Cube, 40 ft open top, 40 ft flat rack, 45 ft High Cube)
